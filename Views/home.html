About us

"Welcome to Circheal, where the mesmerizing world of mandala art and playful doodles come together to ignite creativity and inspire connections. With a passion for intricate designs and boundless imagination,

"we offer a range of immersive workshops, classes, and captivating artwork that celebrate the beauty of circles and lines. Our artistic journey is fueled by a dedication to crafting meaningful experiences.

Mandala Art Workshop:
  1. Discover Mandalas: Explore the history, symbolism, and significance of mandalas.
  2. Create Harmonious Designs: Learn to design balanced and captivating mandalas.
  3. Color Your World: Dive into the world of colors and their emotional impact.
  4. Personalize Your Mandalas: Craft mandalas with themes close to your heart.
  5. Meditative Creation: Experience the meditative and mindful aspect of mandala art.

Bottle Painting Workshop:

  1. Transforming Glass: Discover the art of transforming plain bottles into vibrant artworks.
  2. Painting Techniques: Master techniques like layering, blending, and detailing.
  3. Design Aesthetics: Explore themes and designs that suit bottle canvases.
  4. Light and Shadow: Learn to play with light and shadow for a dynamic effect.
  5. Functional Art: Create stunning painted bottles that can be used as decor or gifts.

Doodle Art Workshop:

  1. Master the Basics: Learn fundamental doodle patterns and techniques.
  2. Unlock Your Creativity: Create personalized and intricate doodle designs.
  3. Integrate Words and Symbols: Seamlessly blend words and quotes into your doodles.
  4. Explore Themes: Infuse your doodles with emotions, nature, and more.
  5. Doodle Challenges: Overcome creative blocks through exhilarating challenges.
