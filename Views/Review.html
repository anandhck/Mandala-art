Asha's Transformation Through Mandalas:

Asha, a stressed-out corporate professional, stumbled upon Circ Heal Studio's Mandala Art Workshop. Through the intricate art of mandala-making, she found a meditative escape from her daily pressures. Over time, her mandalas transformed from mere designs to profound reflections of her inner journey. Today, Asha not only creates breath-taking mandalas but also helps others discover the therapeutic power of art.
